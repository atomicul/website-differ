We’re turning the financial technology landscape into one that feels like home. No more painful rip-and-replace ‘digital transformation journeys’. Just a low-code, plug and play approach that lets our products work for you - out of the box. We empower banks and insurers to put their customers first with unique solutions and products.

The best part? We make it happen in weeks, not months.

Social media, move over. Banking and Insurance are getting personal too.

Customers expect seamless experiences, frictionless interfaces and intuitive data flows - while retaining the ‘human touch’. Banking, financial services and insurance companies are learning that they need to understand their customers as people first, clients second. No easy feat - but with FintechOS’ evolutive data core and ability to conceive and integrate a multitude of data points from your own systems and third parties, you’ll stay ahead of the pack when it comes to data enriched decision making, personalized customer journeys and tailored offerings.

Start small, grow big. Scale at the pace you need.

The first step is the biggest step. FintechOS is designed to build end-to-end customer journeys using a modular set of automation blocks. You pick and choose what you want. If you can think it, you can build it. Our modular approach means you keep your operational resilience - no risk, no downtime and no loss of business continuity. Our solutions are cost effective and designed to scale with you - wherever you set your horizons.

Free your IT.

FintechOS was created for people with a non technical background to build new solutions, leaving your IT team burden free to do the things they need to. Low complexity, code free solutions make space for collaboration between CX managers, data scientists and product managers. With continuous innovation as the norm, your teams are free to build products your customers will love.

A changing financial landscape demands a trusted partner

The financial sector is under unprecedented pressure. From all sides, competition is heating up as new products, solutions and ways to engage with customers take the market by storm.

The march towards digitalization is undergoing rapid acceleration and using outdated IT is fast going from a nuisance to an unjustifiable risk.

FintechOS is trusted by leading financial institutions to bridge the gap between legacy and future-forward approaches - with end-to-end solutions, automated processes, and personalized offerings, keeping customers at the core.

What the industry needs:

Digitized and end-to-end services

Automation to replace costly analog processes

Customer-centricity that delights users

  • Retail Banking

    Build data-driven banking around your customers. The FintechOS platform allows for retail banks to respond to their client needs with tailored products and services. Easily, affordably and at speed.

  • SME Banking

    SME customers expect bespoke business banking tailored to their needs. FintechOS offers personalization and automation tools, which can help growth journeys and make the leap from bank to business partner.

  • General Insurance

    The insurance market is moving towards coverage around data-driven, dynamic profiles and tailored pricing to match changes in risk exposure. FintechOS’ Evolutive Data Layer been built to integrate multiple data streams and is powered by advanced analytics, helping to gauge real-time risk monitoring and enable preemptive outreach.

  • Life Insurance

    Low engagement, high operating costs and product-centric design are the challenges that life insurance providers are facing at a growing rate. Ensuring that an insurance product’s offering remains relevant, investment in technology is critical. Real-time and automated interactions will stop customers from abandoning enrolment processes. Using a data-driven, dynamic profile means that life events can trigger personalized offers on a customer’s preferred touchpoint and drive growth.

  • Health Insurance

    Health insurance providers are susceptible to very similar shifts in consumer demands as retail banking. Health insurers will need to provide digital and end-to-end tailored, personalized solutions to meet the demand of working alongside policyholders as health partners. Personalized content shared must fit the lifestyle needs of policyholders.

Tailored solutions - for financial businesses of all shapes and sizes

Whether you’re a large institution looking for end-to-end change in the way you relate to your customers, or a leading insurance provider that wants to carve out a niche with a custom solution - or a scaling fintech looking for a technology partner - we can adapt to your size, market and needs.
