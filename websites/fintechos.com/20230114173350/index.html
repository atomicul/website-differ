How FintechOS is different

Regain control of digital product delivery

Self-service low-code, no-code capabilities and solution-specific accelerators empower non-technical teams while removing complexity and cost. Reduce your reliance on scarce tech talent.

Go beyond traditional products and services

Flexible product definition capabilities bring together financial and non-financial offerings. Define differentiated products and services across savings, lending, embedded finance, life and health, and general insurance.

Harness innovation from your ecosystem

Prebuilt connectors and integration capabilities enable you to leverage any third-party service. From niche financial services to AI/ML, incorporate the latest innovation into your solutions.

Make data your new core

Data aggregation unlocks the potential of data buried away in existing systems, bringing it into a common layer where it can be enriched and used to personalize products and services.

Automate end-to-end customer journeys

Automation lets you launch new financial products with journeys and experiences that delight customers and with servicing that drives increased operational efficiency.

Start anywhere, make anything

Composability means you can integrate new capabilities with your existing technology investments. Transform your business lines efficiently, explore new markets, and reach value sooner.
